Welcome to HariKrishnan Photography

At HariKrishnan Photography, we believe every photograph tells a story. Whether it’s a wedding, a family portrait, or a milestone celebration, our passion lies in capturing the emotion behind every moment.

With a keen eye for detail and years of experience behind the lens, we strive to create timeless imagery that resonates. Explore our work and let us bring your vision to life—one frame at a time.

I’m HariKrishnan

I’m a passionate photographer based in INDIA. What started as a creative hobby has evolved into a meaningful career capturing moments that matter. With every click, I strive to preserve emotions, stories, and beauty that will last a lifetime.

My Philosophy

Photography isn’t just about taking pictures—it’s about telling your story. I focus on authenticity and connection, letting every frame reflect the essence of who you are and the moments you cherish most.

Why I Do This

Photography is more than just images; it’s a way to preserve emotions, freeze time, and tell stories that matter.

I’m passionate about helping people relive their most meaningful moments through beautifully crafted photos. When we connect like friends, it brings out the best in every photo — candid, comfortable, and real.
